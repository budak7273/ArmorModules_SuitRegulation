# PowerSuit Modules: Suit Regulation

_Modules that allow you to fine-tune the operation of your Suit's Power and Fuel systems._

This is an addon mod for [**PowerSuit Modules**](https://ficsit.app/mod/BezrE8aswqXLRX/) that offers numerous modules focused on granting the player additional control over their suit's internal functions. This includes:

- Suit Power storage
- Suit Fuel storage and transfer speed boosts
- Reduce suit Reboot time
- Increase suit Overdraw safety window
- Expanded Suit Fuel options
- ... and more!

This modpage will eventually be updated with more information. In the mean time, you can find it at the page for the base mod.
